Cuba Libre


Bond ordered an Americano and examined the sprinkling of overdressed customers...

- Ian Flemming, Casino Royal Book Series 1953

  • 60ml Dark Rum
  • 1 Lime Wedge
  • Top Coke
  • Glass: Rocks / Old Fashioned
  • Method: Squeeze / Muddle Lime, Build over ice.

Cuba Libre means 'Liberate Cuda' in Spanish.

During the Ten Years war from 1868 soldiers drank a mixture of molasses, rum and lime while fighting for independance.

It's popularity has increased due to Coca Cola and Bacardi Rum.
