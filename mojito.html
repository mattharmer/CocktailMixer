Mojito


My mojito in La Bodeguita, my daiquiri in El Floridita.

- Ernest Hemmingway

  • Handful of Mint leaves
  • 1 Lime in Wedges
  • Sugar to Taste
  • 60ml White Rum
  • Top Soda Water
  • Glass: Highball
  • Garnish: Mint Sprig & Lime Wedge.
  • Method: Roll Lime & cut in wedges. Remove stem from mint, muddle mint, lime & sugar. Build over chushed ice. Stir
